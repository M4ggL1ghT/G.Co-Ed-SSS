Welcome to the Humanities (Arts) Faculty of G.Co-Ed SSS B-3 Paschim Vihar. Our inspiring and dedicated teachers breathe life into history, literature, geography, and the arts, helping students uncover the stories, ideas, and cultures that shape our world. Through lively discussions, creative projects, and thoughtful exploration, they nurture empathy, critical perspective, and eloquent expression, empowering every learner to understand the past, engage with the present, and imagine a better future.
