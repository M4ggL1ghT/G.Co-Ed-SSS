Frequently Asked Questions

Everything parents want to know

What is the admission process for Class 6th and 11th?

Admissions are based on the Delhi Government guidelines. Class 6th follows the draw of lots system via the DOE portal. For Class 11th (Science/Commerce/Arts), admission is merit-based as per Class 10th results. Online registration opens in May–June every year.

What documents are required for admission?

• Birth Certificate • Aadhaar Card (Student & Parent) • Residence Proof • Previous School Marksheet & TC • Passport-size photos (4) • Caste Certificate (if applicable)

Is there any fee for admission or monthly tuition?

No. This is a Government School under Directorate of Education, Delhi. There is NO admission fee, NO monthly tuition fee, and NO hidden charges. Education is completely free from Class 6th to 12th.

What streams are available in Class 11th–12th?

We offer all three streams:
Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Mathematics, Computer Science)
Commerce (Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics)
Arts/Humanities (History, Political Science, Geography, Hindi, etc.)

What are the school timings?

Morning Shift (Class 6th–8th): 7:30 AM – 1:10 PM
General Shift (Class 9th–12th): 8:30 AM – 2:40 PM
(Timings may vary slightly as per DOE circular)
