At G.CO-ED SSS B-3 Paschim Vihar, we stand as a beacon of intellectual curiosity and moral fortitude. Our principal Mr. Ajeet Singh Kataria and Vice Principal Ms Neetu Kalra believe that the pursuit of truth remains inseparable from the cultivation of character. We champion rigorous scholarship, fearless inquiry, and the relentless questioning of received wisdom, while nurturing empathy, integrity, and an unwavering commitment to the common good.

Here, innovation emerges not as an end in itself but as the natural fruit of disciplined minds and compassionate hearts. Every classroom, laboratory, and playing field serves as sacred ground for the forging of future pioneers—bold, principled, and prepared to advance the frontiers of human understanding and human dignity alike.

This is not merely a school; it is a living covenant between past discovery and future possibility.

Our Core Values

A. Fearless Intellectual Inquiry
We encourage students to question everything relentlessly and pursue truth without deference to popularity or comfort.
B. Moral Courage
We teach that integrity means standing for what is right even when it is costly or unpopular. Character is forged not in agreement, but in principled resistance.
C. Relentless Excellence
Mediocrity is never accepted; every task, from equations to essays, must reflect the highest personal standard. Excellence is a discipline of the will, not a gift of talent.
D. Compassionate Leadership
True leaders place service above self and wield influence to uplift rather than dominate. Power without empathy is tyranny in training.
E. Stewardship of Knowledge
Learning is never hoarded for personal gain but shared as a sacred trust for the advancement of all humanity. Each graduate is a torchbearer, not merely a degree-holder.
