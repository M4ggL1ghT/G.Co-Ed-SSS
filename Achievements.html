Co-Curricular & Extracurricular Achievements

Our students actively participate in zonal and district-level competitions organized by the Directorate of Education, Delhi. We take pride in their contributions to sports, cultural events, science exhibitions, debates, quizzes, and community service initiatives. Through dedication and teamwork, our learners develop skills beyond the classroom, fostering holistic growth and preparing them to become responsible citizens. The school continues to encourage participation in inter-school activities, celebrating every effort and achievement that makes us proud.
