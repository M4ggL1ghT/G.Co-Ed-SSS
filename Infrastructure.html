At Govt. Co-Ed Sr. Sec. School B-3 Paschim Vihar, we believe that a conducive and modern learning environment is key to nurturing young minds. Our campus is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities that support academic excellence, physical development, and creative expression.

Spacious Classrooms

Well-ventilated, spacious classrooms equipped with projectors, and comfortable seating.

Science Laboratories

Fully equipped Physics, Chemistry & Biology labs with modern apparatus for practical learning.

Computer Lab

High-speed internet, latest computers, and software to prepare students for the digital world.

Rich Library

A vast collection of academic books, reference materials, newspapers, and magazines.

Sports Facilities

Large playground for cricket, football, volleyball, and indoor games like badminton & table tennis.

Auditorium & Activity Room

Spacious hall for morning assembly, cultural events, seminars, and annual functions.
